Biography:

Sergio Ramos is a Spanish professional footballer, widely regarded as one of the greatest defenders in football history. Known for his leadership, physicality, and goal-scoring ability as a defender, Ramos has had a long and successful career with Real Madrid, Paris Saint-Germain, and the Spain national team. Ramos spent the majority of his career at Real Madrid, where he won five La Liga titles and four UEFA Champions League trophies. He was pivotal in Real Madrid's dominance during the 2010s and is also remembered for his leadership during Spain's golden era, helping them win two European Championships (2008, 2012) and a FIFA World Cup (2010). Throughout his career, Ramos has played more than 900 matches, demonstrating incredible consistency and a knack for scoring crucial goals, despite being a defender. With over 100 career goals, he holds the record for the most goals by a defender. His aggressive style of play, combined with technical ability, has made him a fearsome opponent and a reliable leader on the field. Ramos has also been the captain for both Real Madrid and Spain, earning numerous individual accolades, including multiple selections to the FIFA FIFPro World XI. His legacy as one of the best defenders in modern football is firmly established.
